Fujairah

Fujairah, the only emirate of the United Arab Emirates located entirely on the east coast, is a destination of natural beauty and cultural charm. Known for its rugged mountains, pristine beaches, and crystal-clear waters, Fujairah is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts and divers. Visitors can explore historical landmarks like the Al Bidyah Mosque, the oldest mosque in the UAE, and Fujairah Fort, a symbol of the emirate's rich heritage. With its tranquil vibe, lush wadis, and opportunities for adventure and relaxation, Fujairah offers a refreshing escape from the urban hustle.
